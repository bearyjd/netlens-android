Security hardening release.

The AbuseIPDB API key is now encrypted on device. Android Auto Backup is disabled so adb backup cannot exfiltrate stored secrets. Pro entitlement no longer silently downgrades to plaintext on a degraded keystore.

The netlens:// deep-link filter no longer accepts launches from browsers or other apps; widgets and the Quick Settings tile keep working unchanged.

Defense-in-depth argv handling for ping and traceroute. Gradle build pipeline pinned by SHA-256.
